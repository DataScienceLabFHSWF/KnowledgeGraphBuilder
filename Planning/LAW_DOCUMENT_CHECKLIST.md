# Legal Document Checklist for Nuclear Decommissioning Law Graph

Gather the following documents (preferably in structured XML/JSON, otherwise high-quality PDF/HTML):

## Core Laws
- [ ] Atomgesetz (AtG) – Atomic Energy Act
- [ ] Strahlenschutzverordnung (StrlSchV) – Radiation Protection Ordinance
- [ ] Strahlenschutzgesetz (StrlSchG) – Radiation Protection Act

## Related Laws/Regulations
- [ ] Gentechnikgesetz (GenTG) – Genetic Engineering Act (if relevant)
- [ ] Bundes-Immissionsschutzgesetz (BImSchG) – Federal Immission Control Act (if relevant)
- [ ] Other nuclear-related federal/state laws

## Amendments & Historical Versions
- [ ] All amendments to the above laws
- [ ] Historical versions (if available)

## Regulatory Guidelines
- [ ] Official guidelines from BMU, BfS, or other authorities
- [ ] Technical rules and standards (e.g., KTA, DIN)

## Metadata
- [ ] Source URLs or official publication references
- [ ] Date of publication and amendments
- [ ] Document language/version

## Format
- [ ] Prefer XML/JSON (e.g., from gesetze-im-internet.de)
- [ ] If not available, use high-quality PDF/HTML
